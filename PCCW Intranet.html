To: All employees
From: Susanna Hui, Group Managing Director
Date: 30 April 2024

 

Reward Yourself, Recharge the City

 

Rooted in Hong Kong with over 150 years of history, the Group is dedicated to strengthening the fabric of our community. With the retail and hospitality sectors facing testing times, it is important to remind ourselves of our responsibility: As an organisation with over 10,000 employees, we should look within ourselves to muster support for our city’s businesses, especially SMEs.

 

On 3 May, we will be launching an initiative designed to bolster our local merchants. As a head start, we would like to first inspire our very own colleagues:
 

  • Hong Kong-based permanent full-time employees1 are invited to leave work at 4pm and enjoy an early Friday evening. 
     
    As you would appreciate, to maintain normal operations and the standard of our customer service, we will be counting on our managers to facilitate the necessary arrangements for colleagues on duty.
     
  • HK$100 will be given to Hong Kong-based permanent full-time employees1 as a small token of encouragement to support the economy.
     
    Staff members can present their local spending receipt(s) to appointed BU administrative staff2 between 3 and 13 May (both dates inclusive) for reimbursement3.
     

Let us head out, have a great time and recharge our city!

 

 

Notes:

1.  All Hong Kong-based permanent full-time employees on Connect as at 3 May 2024

2.  Appointed BU administrative staff will be notified by their respective people managers

3.  Reimbursement subject to production of valid Hong Kong employee staff card
